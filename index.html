Guide to Make Treenet

A treenet is a network of ropes or nets suspended between trees, often used for climbing, recreation, or relaxation. It can serve as a hammock-like lounging area, an elevated walkway, or a canopy play structure. These rope structures are carefully anchored to trees without harming them, using slings or padding to distribute weight. Treenets are popular in eco-friendly outdoor parks, festivals, and forest retreats. They provide a unique way to experience the forest canopy while minimizing environmental impact.

Materials:

  • 325lb Paracord
  • Strong STATIC rope
  • Scissors

Steps:

  1. Wrap trees with your static rope
  2. Tighten rope around trees like crazy
  3. Weave in inside of perimeter rope

You have finished the treenet!
